UTDallas Eats Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how UTDallas Eats (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the UTDallas Eats mobile application and related services (collectively, the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide to us

When you use UTDallas Eats, we may collect:

  • Account information: your name, UTDallas email address, and role (buyer or seller).
  • Profile information: optional details you choose to add to your profile.
  • Order information: details about orders you place or fulfill, including pricing and status.

1.2 Payment and payout information (via Stripe)

We use Stripe to process payments from buyers and payouts to sellers. When you enter payment card details or connect a bank account, that information is collected and processed directly by Stripe. We do not store your full card number or bank account credentials on our servers.

Stripe may collect:

  • Payment card details and billing information.
  • Bank account details for payouts (via Stripe Financial Connections or other methods).
  • Transaction details (amounts, timestamps, identifiers).

Stripe acts as our payment processor and payout provider. Their use of your data is governed by the Stripe Privacy Policy.

1.3 Automatically collected information

When you use the app, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version).
  • Log data (IP address, timestamps, error logs).
  • Usage data (screens viewed, basic interaction events) for debugging and improving the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the UTDallas Eats app.
  • Facilitate orders between buyers and sellers.
  • Process payments and payouts through Stripe.
  • Calculate and track taxes using Stripe Tax (we remain responsible for remitting taxes).
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • Analyze usage and improve the Service.
  • Communicate with you about your account, orders, and important updates.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers such as Stripe (payments, payouts, and tax calculations) and Supabase (authentication, database, and backend services), solely to operate the Service.
  • UTDallas Eats team members who need access to maintain and support the app.
  • Legal or safety reasons if required to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store data using trusted cloud providers. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Sensitive payment and bank information is handled by Stripe. We rely on Stripe’s security controls and do not store your full payment card or bank account credentials.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with our legal obligations (including tax and accounting requirements), resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

6. Your Choices and Rights

  • You may update or correct your profile information in the app.
  • You may disconnect your bank account or stop using the Service at any time.
  • You may request that we delete your account, subject to our legal obligations to retain certain records (for example, transaction data for tax and accounting).

7. Children’s Privacy

UTDallas Eats is intended for university students who meet the eligibility requirements of our platform. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. International Use

UTDallas Eats is currently intended for use within the United States. By using the Service, you understand that your information may be processed and stored in the United States.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top. Your continued use of the Service after any changes means you accept the revised policy.
